Aang is a 12 year old boy, the last surviving airbender, and the Avatar.

Katara is a waterbender who discovers Aang trapped inside a century-old iceberg.

Sokka, Katara's older brother, is a nonbender but is a great strategist.

Toph, a blind earthbender master, is the youngest in Team Avatar.

Zuko, firebender and nation's exiled prince, chases after the avatar to defeat him.

Azula, Zuko's sister and extremely powerful firebender, is next in line to the throne.
